5. Comfort in Discomfort

26/03/2023

I have observed myself in the past few weeks. Whenever I come across anything that is remotely uncomfortable,


  • effort wise
  • or difficult to accept truths
  • or just something I don't agree with

I find myself trying to get busy with something else. Never actually sitting and trying to process the discomfort.


I would cook or watch a series or do something to distract myself.


Like for example: Literally right now, I am writing about this because I don't want to think about something which makes me anxious.


Maybe that's life? Maybe finding comfort in discomfort is the ultimate goal of life? Maybe that's what moving on means?


I don't know. Nuff' said. I'll go back to writing assignments for now...
