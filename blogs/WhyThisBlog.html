1. Why this blog?

07/02/2022

This blog is going to act as a repository of my thoughts and experiences.

I seldom write. I do not enjoy the process of writing, mainly because it takes efforts to organize your thoughts and make them presentable enough not only for myself, but also for someone I am trying to communicate with and I am a very lazy person when it comes to document stuff.

Be it ideas, projects or experiences in general, I am very inconsistent in documenting stuff. And I wish to fix it, this year. It is one of the things I'd like to fix in 2022 .

So essentially, this is my attempt at fixing my writing skills.
